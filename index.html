About This Calculator

This tool uses a validated model to predict the Whole Time Equivalent (WTE) staffing required to run a stable rota. It accounts for two key inefficiency factors:

  • Fragmentation & Severity: This combined factor models the cost of workforce fragmentation on a high-complexity rota. It now uses a blended formula to accurately predict gaps in systems with both low and high numbers of LTFT staff.
  • Rigidity ($R$): This factor models the inefficiency of pre-planned rotas. The penalty now scales with fragmentation, correctly predicting zero planned gaps for a perfectly-staffed, full-time rigid rota.
