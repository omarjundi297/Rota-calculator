About This Calculator

This tool uses a validated model to predict the Whole Time Equivalent (WTE) staffing required to run a stable rota. It accounts for three key inefficiency factors:

  • Fragmentation ($F_i$): A higher ratio of doctors to WTE increases inefficiency due to the greater number of individual scheduling constraints.
  • Severity ($S$): Assumes a high-complexity rota (12hr shifts with multi-day rest), which is more fragile and requires a larger WTE buffer.
  • Rigidity ($R$): Rigid, pre-planned rotas are less efficient than flexible systems. Pooling staff across rotas is the most efficient system.
